(9) Assign operations personnel and crane operators for joint testing during commissioning. (10) Make available weighbridge operators, yard supervisors, and QC personnel for integration testing. (11) Configure L2 and ERP/MES systems on their respective sides to support interface requirements.
Never invent: Obligations for systems not named in project metadata.
Structure: Bulleted imperative list ("Responsible for...", "Arrange...", "Provide...", "Ensure...", "Configure..."). Each item specific enough to be referenced in a project dispute.
