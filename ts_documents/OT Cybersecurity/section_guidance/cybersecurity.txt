## cybersecurity
Role: Formally states cybersecurity responsibilities and Seller liability limitation as a meta-statement covering the delivered OT Cybersecurity solution itself.
Required: Standard OTCSS cybersecurity disclaimer content — no project-specific additions.
Never invent: Novel legal clauses; cybersecurity guarantees; product-level security certifications not confirmed; specific patch timelines or detection SLAs.
Structure: 3–4 short paragraphs: (1) Seller strongly recommends timely implementation of security updates; unsupported versions or missed patches increase cyber exposure. (2) Buyer is solely responsible for preventing unauthorized access to existing plant systems and for ensuring Buyer's users follow established cybersecurity guidelines; if guidelines are not followed by Buyer's users, Seller is not liable for any cybersecurity incidents, data breaches, unauthorized access, or related damages regardless of cause. (3) This implementation contains confidential information — Buyer is responsible for maintaining confidentiality and proper usage. (4) NDA required between Buyer and Seller before any remote support session commences. Use approved boilerplate language only.
