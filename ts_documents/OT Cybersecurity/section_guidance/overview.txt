## overview
Role: Business context and problem statement — why OTCSS is needed, what the current OT security state is, and what the solution delivers.
Required: [CUSTOMER_NAME], [SITE_NAME], [PRODUCTION_ZONE], [PLANT_LIST].

OTCSS as the solution to establish visibility, governance, detection, and documented policy. Tangible and intangible benefit lists.

Include: process_summary (1–2 sentences capturing the enquiry context and scope), system_objective (current OT security problem + OTCSS purpose), existing_system (from metadata; placeholders for unknowns — describe as isolated OT networks with limited visibility and uncontrolled access), integration_approach (OTCSS deployed passively on top of existing OT infrastructure — zero modification to automation systems), tangible_benefits (bullet list — e.g., centralized asset visibility, governed vendor access, continuous vulnerability awareness, real-time threat detection), intangible_benefits (bullet list — e.g., ISA/IEC 62443 alignment, reduced OT attack surface, documented cybersecurity posture, improved operational confidence).
