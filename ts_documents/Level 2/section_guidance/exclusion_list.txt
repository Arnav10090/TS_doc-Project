Structure: Bulleted exclusion list. Standard L2 PMS exclusions: interfaces with systems not named in TS; OS patches/antivirus updates after delivery; source code of core L2 platform; modifications to existing L1, legacy L2, MES, or L3/ERP systems; erection and civil work; mechanical equipment supply or modification; troubleshooting of mechanical/operational/process issues unrelated to L2 application; data-acquisition/historian middleware (where separate product); firewalls and networking infrastructure; performance guarantees on productivity, quality, yield.
Avoid: Inventing project-specific exclusions not in standard list unless in metadata. Do not word exclusions ambiguously — scope disputes arise from unclear language.
Tone: Legal-exclusion list style. Each item clear, specific, and contractually useful.
